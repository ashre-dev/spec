how-it-works

Six steps. Zero middlemen.

ASHRE handles discovery and trust. Money flows directly between buyer and vendor.

01

Vendor lists on ASHRE

Register your business, products, and pricing. ASHRE verifies and publishes you to the registry.

02

AI agent queries ASHRE

The agent asks: "who sells X, ships to Y, under Z?" — structured, machine-readable.

03

Registry returns verified vendors

A ranked list of verified vendors — prices, delivery windows, trust scores.

04

Agent contacts all vendors in parallel

Every matched vendor queried simultaneously. Best deal wins. No bias, no sponsored rankings.

05

Human reviews and approves

The AI presents its recommendation. A human sees the summary and gives the green signal.

✅ human_approval = required
06

Payment goes direct

Money moves straight from buyer to vendor. ASHRE is not in the transaction.
